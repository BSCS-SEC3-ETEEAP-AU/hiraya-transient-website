A SPACE MADE FOR YOU

Experience comfort, style, and convenience in our thoughtfully designed transient space.

🛋 Living Room

A cozy boho-inspired living space designed for relaxation and connection. Featuring warm tones, natural textures, and comfortable seating, it creates a calm and inviting atmosphere perfect for unwinding, reading, or enjoying meaningful moments with family and friends.

🛏️ 2 Connecting Rooms

Enjoy the comfort of two beautifully arranged connecting bedrooms that can accommodate up to 8 pax, perfect for families or groups traveling together. Designed for both convenience and privacy, the space allows easy movement between rooms while providing a cozy and restful atmosphere for every guest.

🍳 Kitchen

A fully equipped kitchen with essential cooking tools and appliances, perfect for preparing your favorite meals anytime. Whether you're cooking a quick breakfast or a full family dinner, the space is clean, functional, and ready for your needs.

🏡 Outside

Step into a bright and welcoming outdoor space that blends modern simplicity with a touch of natural charm. The area features clean architectural lines, warm tones, and thoughtfully placed greenery that create a relaxing, boho-inspired vibe. The gated entrance provides added privacy and security, complemented by installed CCTV for your peace of mind throughout your stay.

🍪 Welcome Treats

Enjoy a thoughtful welcome upon arrival with complimentary essentials prepared for your comfort. Fresh towels, tissue, and amenity kits are provided to help you settle in with ease, along with simple touches that create a warm and relaxing first impression.

🎮 Entertainment Room

A fun and relaxing entertainment space equipped with board games, fast Wi-Fi, and a powerful 2HP air conditioner for maximum comfort. Perfect for bonding, game nights, and unwinding during your stay. Karaoke is also available for enjoyable moments with family and friends.
